The Produce Exchange

Our data set was created by the UC Irvine Machine Learning Repository and lists various physical characteristics present in the abalone samples. Several characteristics include age, sex, and weight, but the most important trait for this project was the number of rings within the shell. The larger the number of rings, the more mature and valuable the abalone is. After entering, transforming, and loading the data, we began testing our data and were attempting to obtain an acceptable score for our model, yet we found that the data is not efficient enough to allow us to confidently make a prediction.
